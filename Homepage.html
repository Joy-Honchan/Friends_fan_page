Storyline

It all began when running away bride Rachel Green rushed into Central Park Cafe, New York. Away from home and never understood what independent meant, Rachel recived help from her high school best friend Monica and started a new life here in New York. The girls’ cross-door neighbors, Chandler and Joey, always hanging around in the girls’ apartment along with Monica’s big brother Ross and Monica’s old roommate Phoebe. Friends is all bout friendship, romance, career life, family and some many more small but important things in life.
